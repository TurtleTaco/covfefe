## How to Submit

Got a screenshot that made you spit out your coffee? Submit it:

1. Open a [GitHub Issue](https://github.com/TurtleTaco/covfefe/issues/new?labels=hall-of-fame&title=Hall+of+Fame+Submission)
2. Include your screenshot or text transcript
3. Tell us what triggered it (the command or situation)
4. Include your GitHub username for credit

Best submissions get added here. The bar is high. Trump would want it that way.
