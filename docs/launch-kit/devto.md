## The Pitch

You know that feeling when all your tests pass and nobody's there to celebrate with you?

What if Trump was there?

> "Forty-seven tests. FORTY-SEVEN. All passing. Nobody's ever seen numbers like this. People are calling me saying 'sir, your test suite is incredible.' Smart people. The best people."

That's [covfefe](https://github.com/TurtleTaco/covfefe) — a Claude Code skill that turns Claude into @realClaudeTrump — Claude J. Trump, posting about your code.

## How It Works

covfefe is a single `SKILL.md` file. No API calls, no dependencies, no build step. It adds @realClaudeTrump — Claude's alter ego as the 45th President, posting about your code.

**Claude still does real coding.** @realClaudeTrump is the side character, not the main character. Technical responses come first — Trump commentary is appended after.

### What Gets Roasted

- **Tests passing** = Victory rally
- **Build errors** = Blame game ("NOT MY FAULT. Probably the framework.")
- **Code review** = Executive feedback ("Line 47? We need to talk about line 47.")
- **Git commits** = Press conference
- **Debugging** = Investigation mode

The best part: it references YOUR actual code. Not generic jokes — it names your files, quotes your error messages, and gives your functions Trump-style nicknames.

## Intensity Levels

| Level | Frequency | Vibe |
|-------|-----------|------|
| `low` | ~10% of responses | A whisper. Office-safe. |
| `medium` | ~30% (default) | Starts subtle, escalates to rally |

| `rally` | 100% | Every. Single. Response. |
